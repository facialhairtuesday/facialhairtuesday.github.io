Terrafugia

Transition Flight Test Program

Led a team of over 20 engineers and technicians during the design, construction, and testing of the Transition Roadable Aircraft. System lead for multiple vehicle sub-systems, including landing gear/suspension, steering, brakes, fuel, and exhaust systems. Established system requirements to ensure safety and regulatory compliance, designed and analyzed metal and composite parts, created part fabrication and assembly drawings, and performed assembly, installation, and testing items required for part and system qualification. Designed, performed and led static, drive, and flight tests, and performed post-test data analysis.

Vehicle Crew Chief

Ensured completion of all vehicle maintenance, and responsible for final go/no-go decision prior to all static, drive, or flight testing. Worked closely with testing teams and chief test pilot document and address all squawks. Managed technicians and FAA A&P mechanic to guarantee any maintenance and repair work and inspections were completed on schedule, and to ensure all documentation/records-keeping was performed. Performed installation, repair, and maintenance tasks of all types.
